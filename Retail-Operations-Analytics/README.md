# Retail Operations Analytics

## Business Problem
Retail leadership needs visibility into:
- Store performance
- Revenue trends
- Inventory risks
- Product profitability

The goal is to identify revenue leakage, low-performing stores, and inventory issues.

## Data
This project uses retail sales, store, product, and inventory datasets (open-source).

## Tools Used
- SQL (PostgreSQL / MySQL)
- Power BI

## Analysis Performed
- Sales by store, city, and product
- Inventory turnover and stockout risk
- Product profitability
- Revenue trends

## Business Insights (to be updated)
- Identified underperforming stores
- Highlighted products causing stockouts
- Detected regions with high return rates
